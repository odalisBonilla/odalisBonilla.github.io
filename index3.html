At a young age, Odalis was introduced to art by a man who would spend his time painting in the park.

Her father would take her across the street to go visit the man in the park. this was great for the father

and Odalis because the father saw the painter as a babysitter and while Odalis was there she learned to paint.

However, this would only last for a year until Odalis needed to go back to the United States and start school.

As Odalis grew older she would, later on, lose interest in art for a while until 8th grade. Many of her paintings

are inspired by her family, her community, and mostly her dreams.
